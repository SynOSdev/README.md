# 🔮 GRIMOIRE — Guided Real-time Interactive Mission Operations in Recursive Environments

> *The synOS Cybersecurity Training Platform*

GRIMOIRE is an immersive, gamified cybersecurity education system built into synOS. Players choose a **Faction** and **Role**, then progress through labs, quests, and side quests that teach real-world security skills while building their ideal synOS environment.

## How It Works

1. **Choose Your Faction** — Align with a team philosophy (see [FACTIONS.md](FACTIONS.md))
2. **Select Your Role** — Specialize in a discipline (see [ROLES.md](ROLES.md))
3. **Complete Labs** — Hands-on exercises that teach core skills (see [labs/INDEX.md](labs/INDEX.md))
4. **Earn Rewards** — Each completed lab unlocks tools, configs, and guides
5. **Build Your System** — Labs guide you to construct your ideal synOS setup

## Core Principles

- **Learn by Doing** — Every lab has hands-on practical components
- **Build Your Own** — Each lab includes a "Build Your Own" section where you create tools
- **Progressive Unlocks** — Quest completion auto-installs tools and generates personal guides
- **Post-Game Reports** — Track what you've unlocked, built, and mastered
- **Your Path, Your System** — Faction + Role choices shape your custom synOS environment

## Quick Start

1. Read [FACTIONS.md](FACTIONS.md) and choose your faction
2. Read [ROLES.md](ROLES.md) and select your role
3. Start with **Core Foundation** labs (LAB-001 through LAB-008)
4. Follow your faction's lab track
5. Complete capstone **Build Your Own** labs
6. Explore [SIDE_QUESTS.md](SIDE_QUESTS.md) for bonus challenges
