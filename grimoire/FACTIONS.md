# ⚔️ GRIMOIRE Factions

Choose your faction during the game intro. Your faction determines your primary lab track and shapes your synOS build.

---

## 🔴 Crimson Circuit — Red Team

> *"Break it to understand it. Understand it to secure it."*

**Philosophy:** Offensive security through ethical hacking, penetration testing, and adversary simulation.

**Primary Track:** Labs 009–016
**Specialties:** Exploitation, reconnaissance, social engineering, privilege escalation
**synOS Build Focus:** Attack toolkits, custom exploit frameworks, automated recon pipelines

**Ideal For:** Players who want to think like an attacker and find vulnerabilities before the enemy does.

### Faction Skill Tree
- Recon Mastery → Network Exploitation → Web Attack Chains → Post-Exploitation Ops
- Each node unlocks tools and personal guides for offensive operations

---

## 🔵 Azure Shield — Blue Team

> *"The wall that never sleeps. The eye that never blinks."*

**Philosophy:** Defensive security through monitoring, incident response, forensics, and hardening.

**Primary Track:** Labs 017–024
**Specialties:** SIEM, IDS/IPS, forensics, malware analysis, endpoint protection
**synOS Build Focus:** Security dashboards, automated alerting, forensic toolkits

**Ideal For:** Players who want to defend networks, hunt threats, and respond to incidents.

### Faction Skill Tree
- Network Defense → Detection Engineering → Incident Response → Threat Intelligence
- Each node unlocks defensive tools and monitoring configurations

---

## 🟣 Violet Nexus — Purple Team

> *"Attack and defend as one. The cycle of security."*

**Philosophy:** Combined operations that merge offensive and defensive skills for comprehensive security.

**Primary Track:** Labs 025–030
**Specialties:** ATT&CK mapping, detection validation, adversary emulation, control testing
**synOS Build Focus:** Integrated attack-defense platforms, detection rule engines

**Ideal For:** Players who want mastery of both sides and the ability to validate security end-to-end.

### Faction Skill Tree
- Attack Simulation → Detection Mapping → War Games → Security Validation
- Each node unlocks integrated offense-defense tooling

---

## 👻 Shadow Protocol — Ghost Team

> *"Invisible. Untraceable. Omniscient."*

**Philosophy:** Stealth operations, OSINT, counter-intelligence, and covert analysis.

**Primary Track:** Labs 031–036
**Specialties:** OSINT, dark web monitoring, steganography, OpSec, attribution
**synOS Build Focus:** Anonymous research stations, covert communication tools, OSINT frameworks

**Ideal For:** Players who prefer intelligence gathering, stealth, and operating in the shadows.

### Faction Skill Tree
- OSINT Mastery → Covert Operations → Counter-Intelligence → Threat Actor Profiling
- Each node unlocks stealth and intelligence tools

---

## Faction Selection

Your faction choice affects:
- **Lab progression path** — Which specialized labs you take first
- **Tool unlocks** — Faction-aligned tools are prioritized in your build
- **Side quests** — Faction-specific bonus challenges
- **synOS configuration** — Your final system reflects your faction's philosophy
- **Post-game report** — Personalized based on your faction's focus areas

> **Note:** All faction tracks are accessible to every player. Your faction determines your *primary* path, but you can explore all labs.
